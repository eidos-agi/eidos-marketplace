# Code Of Conduct

Converge follows the Eidos AGI expectation for public technical work:
contributors should be direct, evidence-oriented, and respectful.

## Standards

- Keep review comments specific and actionable.
- Challenge weak technical claims with evidence.
- Do not harass, threaten, or demean contributors.
- Do not publish private data, secrets, or proprietary customer details in examples.

## Enforcement

Report conduct or safety concerns to `hello@eidosagi.com`.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participation when behavior
undermines safe and productive collaboration.
